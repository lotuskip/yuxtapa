Planewalker

pros: decent in melee, good magic resistance, ability can be very useful for team
cons: vulnerable when using ability (and mediocre when not using it)

Planewalker is definitely a team-player's class. Their special ability, mind's eye allows them to "turn into a spectator" and quickly go see far away locations or take a peek behind a wall. However, when they're using this ability and controlling their mind's eye, the PC herself is completely defenceless and needs protection from teammates.

To use the mind's eye, simply hit space. Then you are controlling, instead of your PC, your mind's eye, which is exactly like spectating. You can hit space again to immediately return to controlling your PC.
