Planewalker

pros: decent in melee, good magic resistance, ability can be very useful for the team
cons: vulnerable when using ability (and mediocre if never uses it)

Planewalker is definitely a team-player's class. Their special ability, mind's eye (ME) allows them to "turn into a spectator" and quickly go see far away locations or take a peek behind a wall. However, when they're using this ability and controlling their ME, the PC herself is completely defenceless and needs protection from teammates.

To use ME, simply hit space. Then you are controlling, instead of your PC, your viewpoint, which is exactly like spectating. You can hit space again to immediately return to controlling your PC.

Some miscellaneous notes about using ME:

  • while using ME, the PC's DV is dropped down to 11, making her much easier to hit
  • the ME view is not affected by blindness. In fact, ME can be used to remove blindness (this takes two actions, though: for turning ME on and off)
  • ME can also be used to detect distant traps (just like traps are normally detected; see Traps)
  • if a teammate switches places with you (see Movement) while you are using ME, your view is reset to your PC's location
  • if you take damage from any source, your view is not reset
