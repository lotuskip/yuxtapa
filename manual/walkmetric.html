Walk metric

Distances in yuxtapa are often considered in the so-called 'walk metric', i.e., how many steps it takes to walk from a point to point. Since diagonal steps can be taken, this makes the field of vision seemingly a square, but in fact it is a circle in the walk metric!

In fact, the often seen 'round' field of vision of many roguelikes is quite odd: it looks seemingly right, but it has the semi-hidden effect that you see further away in non-diagonal directions!
