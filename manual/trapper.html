Trapper

pros: fairly strong, good abilities
cons: no magic resistance, no torch

Abilities

  • Set&disarm traps
  • Detect traps
  • Hiding (on tree squares)
  • No terrain penalty
  • Can push boulders

Trappers can leave boobytrap type Traps. The trap it placed at the position of the PC, and placing it takes a few turns. This ability is used by simply hitting space.

If you hit space on a square that already has a trap, you will disarm that trap instead. Note that outsiders looking at you cannot tell which action you are performing. Teammates will of course see a newly planted trap when you move aside, but enemies still can't be certain whether you disarmed a trap or left one.

One particular use of traps is to identify illusory walls: if a trap is placed on an illusory wall tile, any player who knows about the trap will see the trap instead of a wall.

Additionally, trappers can hide on non-lit tree squares ('T' symbol). The rules of hiding are the same as for assassins, except that hiding can only take place when going into a tree square (with assassins it's: a square next to a wall). Note that unlike assassins, trappers cannot backstab enemies from their hiding place; they just hide.

Trappers are great at detecting Traps, and do not suffer a time penalty for walking over rough terrain or swamp.
