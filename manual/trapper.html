Trapper

pros: fairly strong, good abilities
cons: no magic resistance, no torch

Abilities

  • Set traps
  • Detect traps
  • Hiding (on tree squares)
  • No terrain penalty

Trappers can leave boobytrap type Traps. The trap it placed at the position of the PC, and placing it takes a few turns.

Additionally, trappers can hide on non-lit tree squares ('T' symbol). The rules of hiding are the same as for assassins, except that hiding can only take place when going into a tree square. Note that unlike assassins, trappers cannot backstab enemies from their hiding place; they just hide.

Trappers are great at detecting Traps, and do not suffer a time penalty for walking over rough terrain or swamp.
