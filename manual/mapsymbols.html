Map symbols

Given are the 'normal lighting colours'. Sometimes the symbols can be dimmer or lighter, see Vision and hearing.

Entities explained in Objects in the game:

  • O boulder
  • _ boulder source
  • %% corpses
  • + closed door
  • \ open door
  • &&& flags
  • 0 2-way portal
  • A 1-way portal entry
  • V 1-way portal exit
  • ^^^^^ traps
  • T tree
  • t static torch

Static map tiles (some are discussed in Movement, or Map types):

  • # wall
  • |- windows
  • ~ water
  • " marsh
  • ; rough terrain
  • . ground (grass)
  • . road
  • . floor
  • (nothing) chasm

Other entities:

  • @@@ player characters
  • ** a magic missile (see Wizard)
  • -|/\ arrow (see Archer)
  • <>^v a zap (see Combat mage)

Indicatory symbols:

  • !!!!!! sound/voice indicators (see Vision and hearing)
  • X aimer when playing an archer
  • * indicates the path the arrow will fly
