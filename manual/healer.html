Healer

pros: very useful abilities
cons: weak, bad in melee, short LOS-radius

Abilities

  • Heal temmates or self
  • Poison opponents

To use the abilities

  • press space
  • hit a direction key to heal a neighbour in that direction
    • you cannot heal enemies; instead you poison them
    • you can heal yourself by entering '5' as the direction

Healing always restores a single hitpoint.

A poisoned enemy takes 1 point of damage every 40 turns (10 seconds on normal settings). When a healer heals someone, that someone's poisoned-status is removed, as well. Otherwise, it won't disappear on its own until the victim dies.
