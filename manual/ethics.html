Ethics

This is a short listing of rules that every new player should familiarize themselves with. General things about nice behaviour are not repeated here — politeness, fairness, etc. should go without saying.

  • Keep in mind that this is a team game: play for the team
  • If you play a miner you're expected to dig passages when required; if you play a healer you're expected to heal teammates at least when they request it; etc.
  • Only change your team when the team balance requires it
  • In the same vein, do not go spectator in order to find out the layout of the map or to spy on the other team
  • If you go away from your computer for longer than a few minutes, join the spectators
  • Do not flood the message window by shouting constantly. To determine what is too much, imagine yourself actually uttering all the things you shout
  • Do not spawn kill (SK) unless it's explicitly allowed on the server (see spawning)
  • Do your best not to team kill (TK). Watch it with those arrows and spells
  • Do not cheat, and help to catch cheaters
