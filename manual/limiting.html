Ability limiting logic

The following logic is used to limit the usage of the blinking ability of mindcrafters, and the spellcasting of wizards and combat magi.

There is a counter, call it C, attached to each PC. It is initially at value 0. When you use a limited ability, C is increased by one and you lose the next 2C turns. If you don't do anything (just wait; a lost turn doesn't count here!), C is decreased by one (down to zero).

So, usually you can use your ability whenever you need to, and you lose two turns. But if you try to use it all of the time, this is what happens:

  • you use it at turn 0
  • you lose turns 1-2 (C=1)
  • you use it again at turn 3
  • you lose turns 4-7 (C=2)
  • you use it again at turn 8
  • you lose turns 9-14 (C=3)
  • etc.

The value of C cannot rise higher than to 10.
