Miner

pros: very high HP, high damage, high PV
cons: no torch, short LOS-radius, low DV, no magic resistance

Abilities

  • Dig through walls
  • Destroy boulders
  • Carve new boulders
  • can push boulders

To dig through a wall, stand next to it, hit space, and then hit the direction to dig into (which should be a wall square). Digging a wall takes a while, makes noise, and if you move before you're finished, you have to start over again (you'll know you're done when the wall disappears, obviously).

Note that digging the outer wall surrounding the map is impossible.

Miners can also destroy boulders (see Objects in the game) by digging them. The boulder needs to be 'blocked' for this to happen (i.e. it must not be pushable from the direction of the digging).

This latter ability can make miners irreplacable in a game where the objective is to destroy boulders. Sometimes some parts of a map — even an initial spawn point — might be inaccessible without a miner to mine a way out.

Finally, Miners can carve new boulders from a boulder source (see Objects in the game). Stand next to the boulder source, hit space, and 'dig into the boulder source'.
