Introduction to yuxtapa

yuxtapa is a console-based real-time multiplayer game taking influences from roguelike games and some teamplay focused first-person-shooter games. It has a loose fantasy setting, but no actual background story.

The game features randomly generated maps and ten quite different character classes to choose from. The focus is very much on teamplay and cooperation.

Screenshots are available on the homepage.

Games that have influenced the design of yuxtapa and deserve a special mention:

  • Roguelikes: ADOM, Dungeon Crawl, DoomRL
  • Multiplayer games: particularly Wolfenstein: Enemy Territory

yuxtapa is available only for Linux-like operating systems. It is open source and actively developed.
