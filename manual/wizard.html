Wizard

pros: spell can be very effective, very high magic resistance
cons: the weakest class, terrible in melee

Abilities

  • Cast magic missiles
  • Self-lighting torch

Wizards' special ability is a spell named magic missile, MM for short. It fires 4 missiles, marked by asterisks, '*', in your facing direction, its opposite direction, and the two normal directions: 

either     or
* *         * 
 @         *@*
* *         * 
Note that a missile can only be created at a non-occupied location. Hence, if there is a wall, a boulder, an enemy, or something else there, no missile is created.

To fire the four MMs, simply hit space. Using the ability is limited as described here.

The missiles behave in the following way:

  • They move 1 tile per turn (as fast as PCs, but aren't slown down by terrain)
  • First they move straight for 1 turns in the direction they were shot
    • They won't travel on the turn they were created; hence it is in principle possible to run into your own MM right after casting it
  • After this they will automatically move towards the nearest enemy
    • If there is no enemy within 15 tiles, they keep moving straight
    • Note that the nearest enemy can change every turn
  • If a missile hits a PC, it deals damage and disappears
  • If a missile hits a wall, a boulder, or another missile, it disappears
  • If a missile hits a tree, it has a fixed chance of disappearing, or it "miss" the tree and continue like nothing happened

Note that there is no pathfinding involved: the MMs will not avoid walls, friendly PCs, or even the wizard herself!

Also note that the MMs don't exactly 'beeline' to the target. Rather, they turn, somewhat slowly you could say. Consider the following example. The '@' is an enemy, who remains still. The direction of the MM, '*', is shown by a bar. 

turn 1   turn 2   turn 3   turn 4   turn 5 ... hits on turn 7
.......  .......  ../....  ..*-...  ...*...
.......  .|.....  .*.....  .......  ....\..
.\...@.  .*...@.  .....@.  .....@.  .....@.
..*....  .......  .......  .......  .......

Another ability of the wizards is to light their torch whenever, not just when standing on a static torch (see Objects in the game) or next to teammate with a lit torch.
