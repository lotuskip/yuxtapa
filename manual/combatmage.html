Combat mage

pros: high magic resistance, deadly ability (when used right)
cons: deadly ability (when used wrong)

Abilities

  • Firing zaps

Zaps are bolt attacks that travel in a straight line, bouncing off of walls and damaging anything they hit. The bouncing mechanism is explained and discussed in Zap bouncing.

To fire the zap, you must do the following:

  • hit space to go into spellcasting mode
  • hit the direction you want to fire to
Note that once space is pressed, there is no cancelling! Casting zaps looks something like this: 
@..>..@
.......
......v

The zapping ability is limited as described here.
