Fighter

pros: great in melee (the whole package: defense, offense, and protection + a great ability to boot)
cons: short LOS-radius, no magic resistance

Abilities

  • Circular attack
  • can push boulders

The circular attack is activated by pressing space and giving a direction. The strike takes two turns to carry out, and will hit four adjacent squares per turn, as if the PC attacks all those directions simultanously.

The attacks moves clockwise starting at the direction the player gave (player hits space + 6): 

          xxx
   @x     x@
  xxx
 turn 1  turn 2
(x: a square that is attacked)

The damage and to-hit of the circular attack are not the same as those of the Fighter's regular melee attack; see Rule mechanics.

It is possible to make only half a circle by moving before the second part of the attack starts. This is almost always preferable to regular melee attacks, assuming you can do it quickly enough.

Warning: the circular attack can damage teammates, as well!
