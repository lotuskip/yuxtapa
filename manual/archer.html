Archer

pros: very high DV, very long LOS-radius, great ability
cons: terrible melee attack, no magic resistance

Abilities

  • Shooting arrows

To shoot an arrow, first go into firing mode by pressing space. Your '@' is replaced by an 'X'; this is the aimer.

Now you move the aimer with the numpad keys you usually use to walk around. You can press '5' to return the aimer to your own position. Press space again when the aimer is over your own position to cancel firing. If you press space when the aimer is somewhere else, you will fire at that location.

The arrows fly at a speed of 3 tiles per turn until they hit something or reach the exact tile you aimed at.

aiming   fired
...X     ....
..*.     ../.
.*..     ....
@...     @...

Arrows can also be used to trigger traps. For this to happen, the arrow must land exactly on the tile where a trap is, and the tile needs to be unoccupied at the time (the trap does not need to be visible for the archer!). The trap isn't guaranteed to be triggered, but the chance is fairly high.
